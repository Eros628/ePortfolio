Beneath the Vines is a story-driven psychological horror game currently in development by BisualBytes, our group team in Software Development. Built using the Ren'Py game engine, the game immerses players in a gripping tale of betrayal, fear, and survival.

Set in a vast, eerie mansion, players take on the role of Ivy, a woman uncovering the horrifying truth about her husband’s family—a murderous cult notorious for its gruesome rituals. Ivy must navigate the mansion, evade cult members, solve puzzles, and make impactful choices that shape the story and lead to multiple endings.

As the team’s programmer, I contributed to creating a seamless and immersive gameplay experience. Beneath the Vines emphasizes atmosphere, narrative depth, and player choice over traditional jump scares.

With its blend of stealth, puzzle-solving, and decision-making, the game aims to offer an engaging and original horror experience while showcasing Filipino creativity in the underexplored visual novel genre.
